# Code Of Conduct

OtoChef aims to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about technical concerns.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Accept maintainer decisions once a direction is chosen.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing issues, reviews, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block participants who violate this code of conduct. Serious concerns should be reported privately to the maintainers.
